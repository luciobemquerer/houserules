# Welcome

Welcome to our Tuart Hill home. If you are reading this, it is because something in you made us want to know you better and share this place with you. We enjoy living and sharing a peaceful, safe, warm and welcoming home.

This is a quick guide to how the house runs. There are a few rules but the most important is to feel welcome and at home, be flexible, helpful and mindful of others.

## Rent and bills

We use **Splitwise** to track everything. You'll be invited to two groups: your **Bedroom group** (rent and bills owed to me) and the **Tuart Hill Bills group** (the actual household bills and your credit towards them).

For full detail on how rent and bills work, see the printed Rent and Bills Guide on the fridge.

When you record a payment in Splitwise, make it match the bank transaction: same person, same amount.

## Wi-Fi

Scan the QR code on the fridge.

## General

- Tidy up shared spaces (living, dining, alfresco, family room) after use. Don't leave personal items lying around.
- **Quiet hours:** 9pm to 6am. Use headphones for music or TV.
- Outdoor shoes off at the door. Indoor slippers are fine.
- Report leaks, broken appliances or anything off straight away so it can be fixed before it gets worse.
- Always ask before inviting guests over. Never share keys, codes or the Wi-Fi password.
- No eating on the sofas or armchairs. No feet on chairs or sofas, they are expensive to clean or replace.
- **Keep the front door locked at all times**, even when you're home. Lock all other doors and windows when leaving and at night before sleep.
- Don't enter another housemate's room without permission.
- No smoking, incense, scented candles or other fragrances inside or within 30 metres of the house.

## Kitchen

- Clean up right after cooking or eating. Leave the kitchen tidier than you found it.
- Avoid cooking between 9pm and 6am.
- Wipe the dining table after every meal. Use placemats, no plates or hot pans directly on the wood. Push chairs back when you leave.
- **Dishwasher:** scrape food off plates first, rinse sticky things (yogurt, curry, peanut butter) before loading. Set the magnet to **Clean** when starting and **Dirty** when emptying. Silicone, bamboo, wooden items, sharp knives and cast iron must not go in.
- Hand wash anything that's not dishwasher-safe. Dry dishes and put them away, don't leave them on the sink top.
- Keep the sink strainer in place. Empty it into the bin and wipe the sink after use. Never let solids down the drain.
- Store all food in the fridge or pantry in sealed containers. Nothing exposed.
- Keep your food and kitchenware on your assigned shelves to keep things fair and unconfused.

## Laundry

- Prefer the clothesline. Use the dryer only if unavoidable.
- The dryer is a heat-pump. **Empty the water tank** before and after every load, and **clean the lint filter** after every load. If either fills up the cycle stops.
- Don't leave clothes drying in common areas.
- Take laundry out of the machine and dryer promptly when the cycle ends.
- Return the shared basket to the laundry right after use.

## Upstairs family room and kitchenette

- Treat the family room with the same respect as the downstairs living: tidy up after use, no eating on the sofas, no feet on chairs.
- The kitchenette is for quick prep and drinks. Wipe down counters after use, take dishes downstairs to the dishwasher.
- Keep noise low up here after 9pm out of respect for everyone.
